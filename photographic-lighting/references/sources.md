# Sources

- Fil Hunter, Steven Biver, and Paul Fuqua, *Light: Science & Magic: An Introduction to Photographic Lighting* (Focal Press / Routledge; local copy from Apple Books, edition unrecorded — verify edition/chapter numbering against your copy before citing pages). Primary source for hard/soft light, reflection families, metal, glass, portrait setup, white-on-white/black-on-black, and location lighting.
- Tony Northrup, *Tony Northrup's DSLR Book: How to Create Stunning Digital Photography* (self-published; local copy from Apple Books, edition unrecorded). Corroborating source for natural light, flash, bounce, fill, portraits, catchlights, and field practice.

Re-extraction note: the working plain-text extractions used during distillation lived in a temporary directory and are not preserved. To verify or add quotes, re-extract text locally from your own copies (any PDF/EPUB-to-text tool), then run `python3 scripts/verify_quotes.py` before quoting exact text. The *Light: Science & Magic* extraction showed OCR/PDF-ligature drift (`Te`, `sofer`, `refection`) — prefer paraphrase when an extracted passage is damaged.
