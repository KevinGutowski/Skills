## Verdict

Create a new top-level `personal-growth` activity skill. The corpus clears the source bar with a coherent task shape: help an individual clarify direction, test fit, design sustainable action, recover, and course-correct. Existing skills cover product decisions, managing reports, workplace influence, and learning design; none owns self-directed life/career reflection.

## Excluded material

- The Futur short imposter/self-acceptance clips: duplicate source family, not independent evidence.
- “Conquering Your Internal Demons”: casual therapy/exposure suggestions plus severe-overwork framing.
- The Futur four-circle ikigai video: insufficient provenance for the popular diagram.
- Sensational “10x,” “be delusional,” and reality-distortion framing: energy without a reliable distinct method.
- Unverified quote attributions, acronym etymologies, monkey-ladder story, and unsupported neuroscience/therapy claims.
- Paid Full Time You course lessons and workbook: not accessed; public framework only.

## Source-to-rule map

| Reusable rule | Sources |
|---|---|
| Build a provisional fit profile from energy, values, conditions, enough, and support. | Lewis; Ryan & Deci |
| Generate several futures and prototype the riskiest assumption. | Stanford Life Design; Lewis 99U |
| Pair a desired future with an internal obstacle and if-then response. | Oettingen / WOOP |
| Diagnose failed action through motivation, ability, and prompt. | Fogg |
| Isolate weak links and seek specific feedback without promising effort guarantees outcomes. | Ludwin; Do; Encina; Macnamara et al. |
| Design recovery and use compassionate accuracy after setbacks. | Sonnentag & Fritz; Neff |
| Decide by learning, feedback, energy, fit, and momentum after a meaningful trial. | Manson/Do; Lewis; Stanford Life Design |
