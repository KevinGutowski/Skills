# Coverage Gaps

Use this file for client-work guidance that needs current legal review, market context, or accepted project examples.

## Candidate gaps

- **Contract/legal review:** Deposits, kill fees, termination clauses, IP transfer, indemnity, payment terms, and jurisdiction require current legal review.
- **Pricing exemplars:** Add anonymized pricing packets only when buyer context, value estimate, scope, risk, and outcome are known.
- **Feedback scripts:** Add scripts only when they have worked in a real session or are source-backed.
- **Client qualification signals:** Record examples of red/yellow/green flags before turning them into a checklist.
- **Boundary with products:** Product pricing, devtool GTM, and indie economics route to `devtools` or `building-in-public`.
