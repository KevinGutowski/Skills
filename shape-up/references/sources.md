# Sources

This source map keeps the process schools explicit. Do not merge them into one generic methodology.

## Basecamp Shape Up

- `shaping.md`, `betting.md`, and `building.md` hold the Basecamp Shape Up cycle: appetite, fat-marker shaping, betting table, six-week cycles, cooldown, scope hammering, and team ownership.

## Extensions and competing schools in `SKILL.md`

- Evil Martians design sprint and design-build overlap articles extend fixed-appetite work for design-as-deliverable and investor/prototype contexts.
- Linear sources cover craft-first, no-handoff/no-PM, taste-led work, remote/project operating model, and quality bar.
- Perri, Klein, and Lean UX sources cover metrics-led strategy, outcome-based roadmaps, assumption validation, and learning loops.
- Nan Yu / Linear, Duolingo, and related product-process talks cover org shape, portfolio bets, and scope/quality relationships.

## Promotion rule

Promote process guidance with its school attached. A Basecamp rule, Linear rule, Evil Martians rule, and metrics-led rule may point in different directions; choose by org shape, team maturity, evidence maturity, and appetite.
